## How to Use LeelaClue

### The Main Screen
The main screen is your central hub:
*   **The Carousel**: Swipe between the two primary rituals: **Daily Wisdom** and **Daily Guidance**.
*   **Activity Indicators**: Icons at the top track your daily progress. A checkmark appears when a ritual is complete.
*   **Daily Practice Button**: Tap the calendar icon at the bottom to view your active mindfulness task.
*   **Diary Button**: Tap the book icon to explore your history.

### Daily Wisdom
A morning alignment to set your intention for the day.
1.  **Charge Energy**: Tap the Daily Wisdom card in the carousel. Press and **hold** the main button.
2.  **Focus**: Hold until the countdown completes and the counter turns green. This ensures you are mentally present.
3.  **Receive**: Read your card's theme, description, and quotes.
4.  **Embody**: Each card includes a **Daily Practice**. Once you have completed this task in your life, tap **"Mark as Done"** to record your progress in your statistics.

### Daily Guidance
A meditative 3-card spread for deeper inquiry into specific life situations.
1.  **Ask a Question**: Type your intention (min. 5 words). Avoid simple Yes/No questions; ask "What..." or "How...".
2.  **Focus & Hold**: Hold the button until the circle completes its cycle.
3.  **Reveal**: Tap each card to reveal its unique perspective:
    *   **Where am I?**: Reflective questions to help you uncover your true inner state.
    *   **What I don't notice**: Questions to help you see hidden influences or subconscious factors.
    *   **What will help**: Questions to guide you toward your own resources or next actions.

## My Diary
All your completed rituals are automatically saved to your Diary for long-term reflection.
*   Tap the **Diary Button** (book icon) on the main screen.
*   Use the toggle at the top to filter between **Wisdom** and **Guidance** entries.
*   Review past cards and questions to reflect on how your situation has evolved.
*   To keep your diary focused, you can delete an entry by swiping it to the left (previous days only).

### My Reflections
Capture your insights directly on any card.
*   Tap the **My Reflection** section or the **Edit** icon on a card's detail page.
*   Store your thoughts, feelings, or "aha!" moments.
*   These reflections are stored with the card in your history, creating a map of your soul's evolution.

## Additional Functions

### Sharing Your Insights
You can share your spread or save it for later.
*   On any result screen or in the Diary, tap the **Share** button.
*   The app generates a beautiful image of your spread including your question and the cards.
*   You can share the spread to the communities under Telegram or Instagram channels found in the **Contact** menu (Mail icon).

### Daily Limit
LeelaClue is designed for deep reflection, not casual gaming.
*   **One Ritual Per Day**: You can perform each ritual once per day.
*   This encourages you to stay with the answer and integrate the insight before asking again.

## Example Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Career Decisions
**Situation**: You have a new job offer but are unsure about leaving your current job.
**Question**: *"What should I consider regarding the new job offer at TechCorp?"*
**The Spread**:
1.  *Where am I?*: **Insecurity**. You might be feeling anxious about your skills.
2.  *What I don't notice*: **Sudharma (Right Action)**. You are actually aligned with your purpose.
3.  *What will help*: **Jnana (Knowledge)**. Gather more facts about the new role to calm your fears.

### Scenario 2: Relationship Conflict
**Situation**: You had a fight with a close friend and don't know how to fix it.
**Question**: *"How can I resolve the misunderstanding with my friend Sarah?"*
**The Spread**:
1.  *Where am I?*: **Ego**. You might be holding onto pride.
2.  *What I don't notice*: **Attachment**. You are afraid of losing the friendship, making you defensive.
3.  *What will help*: **Compassion**. Approach Sarah with empathy, dropping the need to be "right".

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I ask another question today?**
A: To preserve the quality of your reflection, the app limits rituals to once per day. Take time to meditate on the answer.

**Q: Why can't I delete today's entry?**
A: Today's reading is meant to be stayed with. You can delete previous days' entries by swiping them in the Diary.

**Q: Where is my Daily Practice?**
A: Draw your Daily Wisdom card first. Then, tap the calendar icon on the Home screen to see and complete your task.
